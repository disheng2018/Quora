What is the biggest life lesson you have ever learned?

Reven Lee, M.S. in Psychology & Consulting, The Ohio State University (Graduated 2017)

74.7k Views · 1.4k Upvotes

  1. Most people won’t support you until they see it’s popular to support you.

  2. When making any major life decision, remember: If it’s not a “Fxck Yes”, then it’s a “No.”

  3. You are not supposed to be the same person you were a year, month, day, or even five seconds ago. You are designed to grow.

  4. The most powerful way to win an argument is by asking questions. It can make people see the flaws in their logic.

  5. Just because you don’t share it on social media, doesn’t mean you’re not up to big things.

  6. Live it and stay low key. Privacy is everything.

  7. One man’s ‘nah you require too much’ is another man’s ‘that’s all you need?’ Don’t settle. You can find better ones.

Christian Bohn:

#1 is so powerful, so don’t forget people who helped you when you were a nobody.

  • nah: no 不，不是
