What was the best relationship advice you ever got?

Zeeshan Mushtaq, I’m living. Learning. Discovering.

357.7k Views · 12.3k Upvotes

A big, big thank you to my ex for showering me with these beautiful pieces of advice.

  • Never date someone you’re not attracted to. If you’re confused, it’s a NO.
  • Never date someone who has recently come out of a break-up. He/She might be on a rebound. In fact, in most of the cases, they are.
  • Never date someone who’s not emotionally strong as you. You’ll be the one to suffer.
  • Don’t rush things. Rushing things will only lead to more complications. Start slow. Discover each other.
  • If your partner has lied to you on small things, he/she is capable of lying to you on more big things as well. Immediately leave them.
  • Lack of communication destroys a relationship. Always communicate about your problems with your partner. Try to sort them out.
  • And lastly, don’t confuse lust with love.

Peace
