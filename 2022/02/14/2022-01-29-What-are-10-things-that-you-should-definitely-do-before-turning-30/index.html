  1. Marriage is just a part of life.
  2. Being single is Normal thing.
  3. Before entering into Relationship , financial independence is must ,specially for girls.
  4. Dont downgrade any girl for being single even near by 30s.
  5. Dont measure salary to give respect to Guys.
  6. Purchasing a car is a liability, owning site is appreciating asset.
  7. If you have 10 lakhs and are thinking about purchasing of a Car , go for purchasing agricuture land in near by small village instead.
  8. Instead of purchasing Car ,go for Cab, mostly it costs less than maintainace fee of Car per Month.
  9. If your sister wants to study further, let her please.
  10. Dont depend tooo much on your spouse.
  11. Household chores is basic thing , every body should learn and do.
  12. Do have Driving License.
  13. Invest in Liquid funds which you can get liquadated in urgency easily.
  14. Government job is not compulsory.
  15. Dont take Relatives so seriously , the demotivating relatives are passing clouds.
  16. Love making is different from having Sex, if your partner forced to have sex before Marriage Dont accept if you are not ready emotionally.
  17. Take a journey with yourself to New places.
  18. Show emotional attachements to only very very closed ones.
  19. Dont make other people to accept your past, you are right at that situations in your own way.
  20. You are not tooo old.
  21. At this stage , most of the parents get Health issues , take Health Insurance.
  22. If you are not satisfied with your Job, try to do online Business ( Flipkart, Amazon ) by selling local products which is famous in your Home town.
  23. Happiness does not come on a particular day or date or after fulfilling a particular wish, it just lies in one’s mind, try to be happy everyday. Becuase we dont know when we will die.
