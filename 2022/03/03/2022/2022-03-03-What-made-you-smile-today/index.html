What made you smile today?

Let us walk together

23.7k Views · 2.1k Upvotes

Many little girls have admiration for their fathers.

It will be a tough nut to crack when she grows up seeing not all men are like her daddy. This made me smile. No matter your dad is trying to impress you or just be a goofy clown to make you laugh, they are our heroes. She is one proud princess, who knows her daddy will be her forever protector. This is very precious!

Dad showing off his skill to the surprise of his little daughter in Melbourne Australia …(circa - 1940)

▌评论:

Victoria Ng:
I was my dad’s favorite and my parents treated me like a princess growing up. I still feel that this privilege is only made possible because i had good parents. They didn’t have a lot to teach and guide, but they gave us everything we needed. Even when I am grown up my dad still treats me like this princess and pampers me.

▌词汇:

  • a hard/tough nut to crack: a difficult problem or situation 棘手的问题，难办的事: Celtic have lost only once this season and will be a tough nut to crack. 凯尔特人队本赛季只输过一场，不好对付。
  • goofy: /‘guːfɪ/ stupid or silly 愚蠢的，傻的: A goofy grin spread across her face when she saw the card. 她看到卡片，脸上露出了傻傻的笑容。
  • pamper: /‘pæmpə/ to look after someone very kindly, for example by giving them the things that they want and making them feel warm and comfortable 宠，娇惯; 呵护: She spent her childhood as the pampered daughter of a wealthy family. 她出身于一个富裕家庭，从小娇生惯养。
