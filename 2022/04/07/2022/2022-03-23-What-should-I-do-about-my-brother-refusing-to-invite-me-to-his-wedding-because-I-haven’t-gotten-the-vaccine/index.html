What should I do about my brother refusing to invite me to his wedding because I haven’t gotten the vaccine？

Jennifer Larson, studied Nursing at University of South Dakota (1996)

171.5K views · 4K upvotes

If there’s a valid medical reason why you can’t get vaccinated , make sure your brother understands that and plan to wear a mask.

If you are just refusing to be vaccinated, your choices are to either get vaccinated or don’t go to the wedding. Sounds like he’s made that pretty clear.
