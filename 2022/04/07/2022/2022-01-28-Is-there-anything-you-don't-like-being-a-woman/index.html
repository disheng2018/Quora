Is there anything you don’t like being a woman?

Sanyukta, Follow electro alien angelJan 22

96.3k Views · 3.4k Upvotes

  1. Having big boobs. Now, I know people think it’s a gift but no, really no. They are unnecessary & painful obstructions.
  2. The monthly Niagara falls accompanied by loose motion and weird cramps. Periods are ridiculous.
  3. Childbirth is obviously a shitstorm and there are all kinds of idiocies related to pregnancy that a good engineer could have designed out.
  4. Being seen as a baby factory. It’s my womb, I’ll decide if I want to put it to use.
  5. Not feeling safe. I’d love to just be able to go for a walk alone when it’s dark out but the risks are too high.
  6. Waxing, shaving or threading.
  7. Basically, feeling the constant need to look a certain way due to societal pressure.
  8. Having my looks matter more than my work or talent. Not always, but sometimes.
  9. So many boys who wanna be our “friend” aren’t actually looking for friendship but hoping for something more. It feels kinda shitty.
  10. Not being as naturally physically strong, knowing that any man could easily overpower me.
  11. People assuming that me liking other women just means I’m kinky or want a threesome. Please don’t be so dense. I’m just not straight, thank you.
