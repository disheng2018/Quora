How do I politely refuse to give my phone to a small child who asks for it

June Smith, Out of the mouths of babes

668.7K views · 29K upvotes

I had a little girl ask if she could have my phone when I was on a train.

I said, “My mummy said I’m not allowed to give anyone my phone”.

She looked at me and said, “OK”.

Children know that adults have mummies too.
