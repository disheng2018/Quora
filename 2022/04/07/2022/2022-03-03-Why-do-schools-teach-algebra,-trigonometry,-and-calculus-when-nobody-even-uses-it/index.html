Why do schools teach algebra, trigonometry, and calculus when nobody even uses it?

Thomas B Walsh, Author, Blogger, and Retired Fortune 100 Executive

219.1K views · 4.1K Upvotes

There’s an old joke.

Student: Teacher, I don’t see why I have to take Algebra or Trig. Does anybody ever use this stuff?

Teacher: You won’t, but the smart kids will.

I have been retired for a long time now, but I hired a lot of college grads in my day. We received LOTS of resumes.

To pare that stack down, I would look for candidates who had taken math and/or coding classes, not The History of Rock and Roll.
