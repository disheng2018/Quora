What are 10 simple habits that can change my life?

Malcolm Lemmons, Entrepreneur & Author (2017-present)

116.3k Views · 1.4k Upvotes

  1. Drink a glass of water immediately after you wake up.
  2. Read for at least 20 minutes everyday.
  3. Mediate or sit quietly for at least 10 min everyday.
  4. Say to yourself one thing that you’re grateful for when you wake up everyday.
  5. Get some form of exercise for at least 30 min everyday.
  6. Tell someone you love them everyday.
  7. Rewrite your top three goals for the year everyday.
  8. Spend 10 min everyday writing down new ideas.
  9. Focus on eating healthier.
  10. Find joy in the simple things and laugh everyday.

“We become what we repeatedly do.“——Sean Covey
