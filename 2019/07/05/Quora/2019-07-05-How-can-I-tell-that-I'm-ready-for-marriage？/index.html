How can I tell that I’m ready for marriage?

Ali AlShamsi, Researching alternative economic system

45k Views · 9.7k Upvotes

  1. If you and your partner are financially ready.
  2. When you are mentally mature (boys, you hit maturity at 30 and girls, you mature younger).
  3. If you can control your temper and emotions and you can absorb your partner’s emotional abnormalities.
  4. If you can communicate your thoughts very well to your partner and you have an understanding for each other even when one of you is upset.
  5. If you can accept your partner’s downfalls and faults.
  6. If you want children and ready to take their responsibility.
  7. If you know that you won’t be cheating your partner.
  8. If you know your partner for at least 6 months (preferably 2 years). You will spend a life time with them so might as well get to know everything after the honeymoon of 6 months. The first year will be hard but you will begin to understand and adjust with each other well from the second year onward.
  9. If you both are willing to compromise to give up things and change things to adjust with each other. There is no perfect match. You have to create it and people have differences that needs to be adjusted as long as they are not core differences.
  10. If your partner is your best friend.
