    What is your most powerful tip?

    Emily Clark, Supply Chain Manager at Aerospace Industry (2016-present), Updated Fri · Upvoted by Zi Heng Ng, Ph.D. Humanity & Life and Living, Earth (1994)

    275.8k Views · 12.3k Upvotes

    I am sharing the list of Most Powerful Life tips.

    1. When you don’t want to check your bank account because you are afraid, you should definitely check your bank account.
    2. Don’t fill “To” field in email until you are completely done with the content of email.
    3. The period key and comma key move YouTube videos one frame forward and backward respectively.
    4. Be objective instead of being subjective. e.g. if someone send you an email with no attachment then say “attachment is missing in the email” instead of “you did not send attachment along with email”.
    5. Always check the air pressure in your spare tire and learn how to use it before going to long trip.
    6. If you are nervous about public speaking, go first, and do it.
    7. If it takes less than 15 minutes to do then do it now instead of procrastinating it for later.
    8. If you are getting in a large park with your kids, take a picture of your kids, so in case you lost them at park you can show how they look like and what they’re wearing.
    9. Use the “it was my understanding” with any idea or thought when talking professionally.
    10. If your printer ran out of black ink, then change the color of font to #010101, which is 99% grey and it will work fine.
    11. If someone is not shutting up and won’t let you talk, drop something, person will pause when you go down to pick it up. Use that as an opportunity to say your point.
    12. Before doing anything, think “would an idiot do that?” and now you got your answer.
    13. always remember X% of Y is equal to the Y% of X.
    14. Always check the reader first of the gas pump machine before entering your card in it.
    15. In setting put on the “undo” option for email so you have 30 seconds time to unsend the email, you wrongly sent.
    16. Apologize less and say thank you more e.g. say “thank you for listening” instead of saying “I’m sorry i talk too much”.
    17. I never break the hug first with the person I love or getting interested.
    18. If you hate splashing because of poop drop on water, put toilet paper on the top of water in commode.
    19. If someone ask “why are you so quiet?” answer them with “Why, are you nervous?”
    20. Always remember “NO” is a complete sentence.
    21. Sometimes you type something with all CAPS on and you don’t want to rewrite it all. Just select all the text and press Shift + F3
    22. All government universities offer a student email ID, use that to get free Microsoft Windows instead of paying.

    ​ ▌词汇:

    • period key: 句号；
    • comma key: 逗号；
    • objective: not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; unbiased; fair 客观的; 不受个人的感情或意见影响的; 无偏见的；
    • subjective: (of ideas, feelings, etc) existing in the mind and not produced by things outside the mind （指思想、感情等）主观的（属於自我意识方面的）；
