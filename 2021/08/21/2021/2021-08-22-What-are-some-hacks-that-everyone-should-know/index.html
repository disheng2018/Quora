What are some hacks that everyone should know?

Elizabeth Collins

423.6k Views · 15.3k Upvotes

  1. When you sign up for anything online, put the website’s name as your middle name. That way when you receive spam/advert email, you will know who sold your information.

  2. If you’re on a first date and aren’t connecting with the other person or feel they’re dull, ask them what job they’d choose if money wasn’t an issue.

  3. If you want to be an effective communicators, let others talking about themselves and their interests.

  4. Do not try to be the man your father would want you to be. Be the man you would like your son to be be. It more clearly defines your own convictions, desires, goals, and motivates you to be your best.

  5. Pay Attention to the smell of your home when you come back from a trip - that’s what it smells like to guests all the time, you just get used to it.

  6. No matter how much your workplace pushes “team building” and “family culture” - remember, they’re not your friends and it’s still a workplace.

  7. If you’re stuck on an annoying call, put your phone on airplane mode instead of just hanging up. The other person will see “call failed” instead of “call ended”.

  8. If you want to learn a new language, figure out the 100 most frequently used words and start with them. Those words make up about 50% of everyday speech, and should be a very solid basis.

▌翻译:

  1. 当你在网上注册时，把网站的名字作为你的中间名。这样的话，当你收到垃圾邮件/广告邮件时，你就会知道是谁卖了你的信息。
  2. 如果你在第一次约会时没有和对方聊得很好或者觉得对方很闷，可以问对方“如果钱不是问题，他们会选择什么工作”。
  3. 如果你想成为一个有效的沟通者，让别人谈论自己和自己的兴趣。
  4. 不要试图成为你父亲希望你成为的那种人，要成为你希望你的儿子成为的那种人。它更清楚地定义了你自己的信念、欲望、目标，并激励你成为最好的自己。
  5. 注意你旅行回来时家里的气味儿——对客人来说，这就是他们一进门时候你家里的气味儿，你闻不到只是因为你已经习惯了。
  6. 无论你的工作场所如何推崇“团队建设”和“家庭文化”——记住，他们不是你的朋友，这仍然是一个工作场所。
  7. 如果你不想接某个恼人的电话，把你的手机调到飞行模式，而不是直接挂断。对方会看到 “通话失败 “而不是 “通话结束”。
  8. 如果你想学习一门新的语言，找出100个最常用的单词并从它们开始。这些词约占日常讲话的50%，这将会是一个非常坚实的基础。
